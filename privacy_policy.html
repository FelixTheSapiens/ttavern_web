Display: The right to display the uploaded images within the TTavern app and on our website

for the purpose of enhancing user interaction and experience.

Modification: The right to resize, format or otherwise modify the uploaded images for the purpose

of standardizing the visual content across the TTavern platform.

Promotion: The right to use uploaded images for promotional activities, including but not limited to,

marketing, advertisements, and social media posts, provided that such use does not violate your privacy

settings within the app.

Analysis: The right to use the uploaded images for analytical purposes, including but not limited to,

improving our services, user engagement, and developing new features.

Sharing: The right to share the images with third-party services and partners that we use

to enhance the functionalities of TTavern, subject to the same level of privacy protections

outlined in our Privacy Policy.


By uploading an image, you confirm that you have the right to distribute this image

and that it does not violate our terms of service, any intellectual property rights, or privacy regulations.


How We Use Collected Images

Images that you upload to TTavern are used for multiple purposes:

User Profile Display: Images are displayed on your user profile to provide

a personalized experience and to help other users identify you within the community.

Game Illustration: Images can be uploaded to illustrate games that you are part

of or organizing, thereby aiding in the description and appeal of the gaming session.

Personalization: We may use the images to enhance your user experience

by offering avatar options or suggestions based on your uploaded images.

In-App Functionality: Images may be used in in-app messaging or discussion forums

to facilitate communication and discussion among users.

Analytics: Aggregated image data may be analyzed to improve app functionality,

user engagement, and to develop new features.

By uploading images, you grant TTavern the rights as outlined in our

Image Data Usage and Rights section.
